All About Rex Orange County

Alex O'Connor, better known as Rex Orange County, is a talented singer/songwriter known for his music. As a teenager he had a passion for music and was self-taught. He learned to play the drums, electric guitar, piano, and production software skills. His music is a mix of bedroom pop, jazz, hip-hop, and soul influences. O'Connor was only a teen when he first gained online attention in 2015 for his self-produced album "bcos u will never be free." Later in 2017, he released "Apricot Princess" which cracked the Top Ten of Billboard's Independent Albums chart. This eventually paved the way for his newest album "Pony" that released in October 2019.

Alex gained recognition from other artists aswell. In 2017, Tyler the Creator featured O'Connor on two tracks from his "Flower Boy" album. In that same year he released popular singles like "Best Friend" and "Sunflower". His new album "Pony" landed him number one on the Billboard Top Rock and Alternative Albums charts.
